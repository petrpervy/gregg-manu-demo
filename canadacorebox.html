The Product

Built for the way
mining really works.

CanadaCoreBox is our flagship line — wooden core sample storage boxes engineered for mineral exploration programs from regional greenfields to major drill campaigns. Made from kiln-dried Canadian spruce with CNC-machined channels, each box is designed to protect your core through transport, storage, and long-term archiving.

  • Kiln-dried Canadian spruce — dimensionally stable, resists warping in field conditions
  • CNC-machined channels — consistent, precise spacing for all core diameters
  • Stackable tapered edges — stable multi-box storage on-site and in transit
  • Custom site-identification stamping available on request
