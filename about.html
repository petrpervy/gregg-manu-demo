Greg's Manufacturing Limited (GML): A Legacy of Excellence

Greg's Manufacturing Limited (GML) is a family-owned company with a rich history in craftsmanship, innovation, and precision manufacturing. Established in 1934 by Fred Petryk in Cranberry Portage, Manitoba, GML has grown from a small sawmill into a trusted provider of specialized wood products for the mining, drilling, and construction industries. Now led by the third generation, the company continues to uphold its reputation for quality and reliability.

From Sawmill to Industry Leader

Fred Petryk founded the business with a vision of providing high-quality lumber. Initially, logging was done manually, but as demand increased, mechanization improved efficiency. Over the years, Fred's dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and integrity helped establish the company as a key regional supplier.

Following Fred's passing, the business became Petryk Forest Products, with leadership transitioning to his wife Anne and their sons, Michael and Rick. The company modernized operations, expanded its client base, and supplied wood for residential, industrial, and mining applications. At its peak, it employed up to 30 workers, contributing significantly to the local economy.

Greg's Manufacturing crafts all lumber and wood products from locally sourced, ethically harvested Canadian timber. Our expert teams follow sustainable forestry practices to protect the health of our forests.

Specialized Manufacturing for Mining and Drilling

Today, GML specializes in precision wood products tailored for the mining, drilling, and industrial sectors, including:

  • Mouldings – Custom and standard wood profiles
  • Pallets – Durable solutions for industrial transport
  • Core Boxes – Essential for geological drilling
  • Blasting Sticks – Precision tools for mining operations
  • Wedges & Marking Blocks – Industrial measurement and safety solutions
  • Custom Rough Lumber – Tailored for specialized projects

GML supplies major mining companies such as Vale (Thompson, MB), Hudbay (Flin Flon & Snow Lake, MB), and Foran Mine (SK). With an annual production of approximately 500,000 meters of core boxes, the company has proven its ability to meet high industry demands.

Advanced Technology & Sustainable Practices

To maintain precision and efficiency, GML utilizes state-of-the-art woodworking machinery, including:

  • Weinig Moulder – For custom molding and profiling
  • Chop Saw & Band Saw – For precision cutting
  • Rip Saw – For high-accuracy lumber production

GML is also committed to environmental sustainability. It uses ethically sourced Canadian timber and efficient production methods to minimize waste and reduce its environmental footprint.

Looking Forward

As GML looks to the future, the company remains committed to its core values of quality, reliability, and sustainability. The third generation of leadership continues to invest in modernization and innovation while preserving the craftsmanship legacy that Fred Petryk established nearly a century ago.

Today, GML stands as a testament to the enduring power of family business, regional pride, and the pursuit of excellence in manufacturing. We're proud to serve the mining and industrial sectors with precision wood products that make a difference in their operations.
