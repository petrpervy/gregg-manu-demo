Greg's Manufacturing Limited (GML): How We Got Here

Greg's Manufacturing Limited (GML) is a family-owned company started in 1934 by Fred Petryk in Cranberry Portage, Manitoba. We grew from a small sawmill into a wood products supplier for the mining, drilling, and construction industries. Three generations in, we still build it right and keep our word.

From Sawmill to Industry Leader

Fred started the business providing good lumber. Early on, everything was done by hand, but as orders picked up, we added machines to keep up. Fred built the company on doing the work right and treating people fair—that's still how we operate.

Following Fred's passing, the business became Petryk Forest Products, with leadership transitioning to his wife Anne and their sons, Michael and Rick. The company modernized operations, expanded its client base, and supplied wood for residential, industrial, and mining applications. At its peak, it employed up to 30 workers, contributing significantly to the local economy.

Specialized Manufacturing for Mining and Drilling

Today, GML specializes in precision wood products tailored for the mining, drilling, and industrial sectors, including:

  • Mouldings – Custom and standard wood profiles
  • Pallets – Durable solutions for industrial transport
  • Core Boxes – Essential for geological drilling
  • Blasting Sticks – Precision tools for mining operations
  • Wedges & Marking Blocks – Industrial measurement and safety solutions
  • Custom Rough Lumber – Tailored for specialized projects

GML supplies major mining companies such as Vale (Thompson, MB), Hudbay (Flin Flon & Snow Lake, MB), and Foran Mine (SK). With an annual production of approximately 500,000 meters of core boxes, the company has proven its ability to meet high industry demands.

The Equipment

We use modern woodworking machinery to keep precision high and costs down:

  • Weinig Moulder – Custom molding and profiling
  • Chop Saw & Band Saw – Precision cutting
  • Rip Saw – Accurate lumber production
